LORRAINE UGEN

British Olympic Long Jumper

Lorraine Ugen is British Olympic Long Jumper. The 2016 Rio Olympic Games finalist also competed at the 2020 Tokyo Olympic Games. She is a two time World Indoor Championship Bronze Medalist and European Indoor Silver Medalist. Lorraine has competed in three World Championship finals placing 5th place in 2015 and 2017. Not only is Lorraine a Long Jumper, but she also has some speed, stepping into the 2018 Commonwealth Games relay team where Team England claimed Gold. She formerly held the Indoor British Record in the Long Jump.
